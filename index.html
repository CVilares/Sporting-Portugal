About us!

This web page was created with the aim of supporting and promote Sporting Portugal in a free, non profit and passionate way regarding all types of sports practiced in the club. Its for all fans of this club or those interested about getting to know it. In the future we will have daily news, players, pre, mid and end seasons analyzes, the list of trophies in all disciplines, some live broadcasts in connection with sportingTV, all the analyzes about the games and you maybe the lucky one to be present in our Hall of Fame, our future virtual museum !
